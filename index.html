Our Prime Motive

As a trust, our prime motive is not to build profit from our members or clients. Instead, we rely on voluntary donations to support our operations. We encourage the public to avoid using intermediaries, who often lack the empathy needed and may escalate your medical expenses. Share your needs with us, we are here to support you, doing our best to reduce your medical costs while prioritizing compassionate care.
